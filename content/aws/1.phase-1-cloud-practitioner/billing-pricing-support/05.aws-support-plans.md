# AWS Support Plans

AWS offers four tiers of support plans. Every AWS account starts with **Basic Support** at no charge. As your workloads grow and become business-critical, you can upgrade for faster response times, expert guidance, and proactive support.

## Support Plan Comparison

| Feature | Basic | Developer | Business | Enterprise |
|---------|-------|-----------|----------|------------|
| **Price** | Free | From $29/month | From $100/month | From $15,000/month |
| **Best for** | Learning, experimenting | Development and testing | Production workloads | Business-critical workloads |
| **Technical support** | None | Business-hours email | 24/7 phone, chat, email | 24/7 phone, chat, email |
| **Trusted Advisor** | Core checks only | Core checks only | Full checks | Full checks |
| **TAM** | No | No | No | Yes |
| **Response (critical)** | N/A | N/A | 1 hour | 15 minutes |

## Basic Support

Included with **every AWS account** at no cost.

- 24/7 access to customer service (billing and account questions only)
- AWS documentation, whitepapers, and re:Post community forums
- **AWS Health Dashboard** -- shows service status and alerts about events affecting your account
- **Core Trusted Advisor checks** -- a limited set of checks for basic best practices
- **No technical support cases** -- you cannot open a support ticket for technical help

### Developer Support

Starting at **$29/month** (or 3% of monthly AWS usage, whichever is greater).

- Everything in Basic, plus:
- **Technical support via email** during business hours
- **General guidance** -- response within 24 hours
- **System impaired** -- response within 12 hours
- **1 primary contact** can open support cases
- Best for development, testing, and non-production workloads

### Business Support

Starting at **$100/month** (or 3-10% of monthly AWS usage, tiered).

- Everything in Developer, plus:
- **24/7 technical support** via phone, chat, and email
- **Production system impaired** -- response within 4 hours
- **Production system down** -- response within 1 hour
- **Full Trusted Advisor checks** -- all checks across cost, performance, security, fault tolerance, and service limits
- **AWS Support API** -- programmatic access to create and manage support cases
- **Third-party software support** -- help with common OS and application stack issues
- **Unlimited contacts** can open support cases
- AWS recommends this as the **minimum for production workloads**

### Enterprise Support

Starting at **$15,000/month** (or 3-10% of monthly AWS usage, tiered).

- Everything in Business, plus:
- **Business-critical system down** -- response within **15 minutes**
- **Technical Account Manager (TAM)** -- a dedicated AWS expert who knows your architecture and proactively guides you
- **Well-Architected Reviews** -- TAM reviews your workloads against AWS best practices
- **Infrastructure Event Management** -- AWS helps you plan for major events (product launches, migrations)
- **Concierge Support Team** -- billing and account experts for enterprise-level assistance
- **Training and game days** -- access to self-paced labs and operational simulations

## Enterprise On-Ramp

AWS also offers **Enterprise On-Ramp** (starting at $5,500/month) as a step between Business and Enterprise. It includes a pool of TAMs (not a dedicated one), 30-minute response for critical issues, and annual architectural reviews.

## Response Times by Severity

| Severity | Description | Developer | Business | Enterprise |
|----------|-------------|-----------|----------|------------|
| **General guidance** | Usage questions | 24 hours | 24 hours | 24 hours |
| **System impaired** | Non-critical function degraded | 12 hours | 12 hours | 12 hours |
| **Production impaired** | Important function degraded | N/A | 4 hours | 4 hours |
| **Production down** | Business significantly impacted | N/A | 1 hour | 1 hour |
| **Business-critical down** | Business at risk | N/A | N/A | 15 minutes |

## Choosing the Right Plan

| Your Situation | Recommended Plan |
|---------------|-----------------|
| Learning AWS, personal projects | **Basic** (free) |
| Building and testing applications | **Developer** ($29+/month) |
| Running production workloads | **Business** ($100+/month) |
| Mission-critical apps, need a TAM | **Enterprise** ($15,000+/month) |

## Key Takeaways

- **Basic** is free and included with every account -- no technical support, only billing help and documentation
- **Developer** adds email-based technical support during business hours for $29+/month
- **Business** is the minimum for production -- 24/7 phone/chat support, full Trusted Advisor, 1-hour critical response
- **Enterprise** adds a dedicated TAM, 15-minute critical response, and Well-Architected Reviews
- Response times get faster as you move up tiers -- from 24 hours (general) to 15 minutes (business-critical)
- Choose your plan based on how critical your workloads are and how fast you need help
